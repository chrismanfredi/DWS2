This has come at the expense of features previously enjoyed by gamers, like custom tournament and guest play. A number of users have complained of various bugs and glitches which EA developers hope to eradicate through the normal patching process. The momentum (aka scripting) is also prevalent this year, designed to add to the excitement of matches around the 45 and 90 minute marks.

Ignite Engine

A new engine called the Ignite Engine is used in FIFA 14 on the PlayStation 4 and Xbox One versions only. This features both graphical and game-play changes such as human intelligence to make players react more like the real thing, True Player Motion to create more realistic movement from the players and more realism to the way environmental features like weather and stadium crowd dynamics develop during play.[5] A PC port of the version with Ignite Engine was in the works, but eventually it was cancelled.
